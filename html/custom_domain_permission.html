What This Does

  • Enables BES features on this custom Bandcamp domain
  • Allows the extension to read and modify pages on this domain
  • Provides the same features as on *.bandcamp.com domains

Security Warning

⚠️ Only authorize domains you trust

This will give the extension full access to pages on this domain. Only add domains that you know are legitimate Bandcamp artist sites. Never add domains you don't recognize or trust.

Managing Permissions

You can remove this authorization at any time from the BES settings drawer by clicking the × button next to the domain.
