Authorize FindMusic.club

Bandcamp Enhancement Suite is partnering with FindMusic.club to provide music discovery features based on your collection.

What We Access

  • Your Bandcamp cookie for authentication only
  • Your public collection data to provide recommendations
  • No passwords or payment information

Privacy & Security

Your credentials are never stored

We only use your Bandcamp cookie to create a secure login token. Your authentication details are used once and discarded.

Managing Permissions

You can revoke this authorization at any time by visiting Chrome's extension settings: chrome://extensions → Bandcamp Enhancement Suite → Permissions → Remove access to cookies.

By authorizing, you agree to FindMusic.club's Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

This integration is also covered in the Bandcamp Enhancement Suite Privacy Policy, which has been updated to reflect this feature.
