Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-06

Steven Bjornson ("us", "we", or "our") operates the Bandcamp Label View chrome extension (the "Extension"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information we receive from users of the Extension.

Information Collection And Use

The Extension does not collect Personal Information except for the data collected by Google with the Chrome Storage API.

Log Data

Data is not logged or stored by the Extension except for the use of the Google Storage API for persistence of user accessible data between sessions. We do not have access to this data.

FindMusic.club Integration

The Extension offers optional integration with FindMusic.club, a third-party music recommendation service. When you choose to use this feature and grant the Extension permission to access cookies:

  • Your Bandcamp authentication cookie is sent to FindMusic.club for the purpose of logging you in
  • FindMusic.club may access your public Bandcamp collection data to provide music recommendations
  • This data sharing only occurs after you explicitly grant cookie permissions through the Extension's authorization dialog
  • You can revoke these permissions at any time through Chrome's extension settings

FindMusic.club is operated by Bjornsoft and has its own privacy policy. By authorizing the FindMusic.club integration, you agree to FindMusic.club's Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

Communications

We do not have access to any of your Personal Information.
